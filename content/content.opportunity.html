Senior project back to top

 Student may contact any of the PIs for information on currently available projects and to discuss his/her interests. We can accommodate student with diverse background (biology, mathematics, computer science, or engineering) but basic programming skills and knowledge in statistical or machine learning techniques will be particularly helpful. Here are some examples of the senior projects or independent studies in our group:

  • Prediction of binding between protein and peptide via deep learning

     We trained deep neural network models for predicting the binding probability between human leukocyte antigen (HLA) proteins and short antigen peptides. More than 200,000 data points from binding experiments and direct sequencing of HLA-bound peptides were used. This project is part of the cancer vaccine initiative at Faculty of Medicine, Chulalongkorn University.

  • Classification of virulent and harmless viral strains from genomic sequence

     We explored the capability of DNA k-mer profiles for distinguishing virulent and harmless strains among closely related viruses and for predicting the direction of viral evolution over time.

  • Automatic curation of bioinformatics tools from literature

     Useful bioinformatics tools get published at an ever increasing rate. To identify the best tools, it is critical to develop a platform that can automatically curate tool-describing publications and evaluate theirs impact. This project is an initial step toward building such system.
