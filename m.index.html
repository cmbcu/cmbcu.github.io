About us

The Computational Molecular Biomedicine group at Chulalongkorn University (CMB@CU) combines thorough understanding of high-throughput technology and molecular biology with cutting-edge computational techniques to challenge complex problems in biomedicine. We are interested in applying computational approaches to improve every aspect of biomedical research – from data acquisition and processing to data analysis and interpretation.
